Crystal Collectors!


Instruction

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are four crystals below, clicking on a crystal will add points to your total score.

To win you need to match your score to the random number. You will lose the game if your score goes above the random number chosen for the round.

Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will differ.
